<div class='bold'>How to:</div> Sweep the field and guess where the 17 mines are. Click on fields with no mine beneath it. Wherever you think there is a mine, place a red flag. Switch between flag setting and empty field discovering by clicking on the repective button on the right. The game ends as soon as you either tap onto a mine or you have marked all fields correctly.
<div class='bold'>How to:</div> Avoid the pipes by pressing the space key continuously. For each pipe that you pass one point is awarded. <div class='bold'>Keys:</div> Space <div class='bold'>Mobile</div> touch
<div class='bold'>How to:</div> Release the ball by pressing space or the up Arrow. Move your pad in order to rebounce the ball. For each destroyed brick one point is awarded. Avoid bombs and collect power-ups that enlargen (green) or tighen (red) your pad. <div class='bold'>Keys:</div> Left & right arrows, space <div class='bold'>Mobile:</div> press on the left and right side to adjust the position of the pad, touch the screen to start
<div class='bold'>How to:</div> Kill the chickens by tapping on them. Mind the gnomes! For each chicken you hit one point is awarded. Each mishit will cost you one life. The game ends as soon as you've lost all of your 10 lives. <div class='bold'>Keys:</div> Right click <div class='bold'>Mobile:</div> touch
<div class='bold'>How to:</div> Avoid the incoming obstacles by jumping or ducking. For each obstacle that you pass one point is awarded. <div class='bold'>Keys:</div> Up & down arrows <div class='bold'>Mobile:</div> touch on upper and lower screen
